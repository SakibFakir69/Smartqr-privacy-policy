
<p>SmartQR ("the app") is developed by Seven Venture Labs. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it's handled.</p>

<h2>Camera</h2>
<p>SmartQR uses your device camera to scan QR codes and barcodes in real time. Camera data is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.</p>

<h2>Link Safety Check</h2>
<p>When you scan a QR code containing a link, SmartQR runs an on-device heuristic to flag it as Safe, Caution, or Suspicious. This check happens locally — no URL or scan content is sent to any server.</p>

<h2>Scan History</h2>
<p>Scanned codes and generated QR codes are saved locally on your device using SQLite. This history is never uploaded, synced, or shared. You can delete individual entries or export your history as a CSV file at any time; that export stays on your device unless you choose to share it yourself.</p>

<h2>Advertising</h2>
<p>SmartQR shows ads via Google AdMob (banners and interstitials). AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) and usage data to serve relevant ads, per <a href="https://policies.google.com/privacy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Google's Privacy Policy</a>.</p>
<p>In the EEA/UK, a consent form (Google User Messaging Platform) appears before any personalized ads are shown, letting you choose your ad preferences.</p>

<h2>In-App Purchase</h2>
<p>SmartQR offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase, processed through Google Play Billing. SmartQR does not receive or store your payment details — Google handles all payment processing.</p>

<h2>Data Not Collected</h2>
<p>SmartQR does not require an account, does not collect your name, email, or location, and does not share scan content, history, or camera data with any third party except as described above for advertising.</p>

<h2>Children's Privacy</h2>
<p>SmartQR does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require sign-up and does not collect personal identifiers beyond advertising data described above.</p>

<h2>Changes to This Policy</h2>
<p>This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent revision.</p>
